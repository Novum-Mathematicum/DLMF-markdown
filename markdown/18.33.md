A system of polynomials $\{\phi_{n}(z)\}$ , $n=0,1,\dots$ , where $\phi_{n}(z)$ is of proper degree $n$ , is *orthonormal on the unit circle with respect to the weight function* $w(z)$ ( $\geq 0$ ) if
